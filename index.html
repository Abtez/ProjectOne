Watermelon is a sweet and refreshing fruit that provides hydration and also essential nutrients, including vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to the body. Among its benefits is that it contains compounds that may help prevent cancer and improve your heart health.Antioxidants help lower inflammation and oxidative stress.This fruit also helps prevent Macular Degeneration and relieves muscle soreness.

Ever wondered what goodness is in a banana? Other than being rich in vitamin B6, bananas are a good source of vitamin C, which helps in the growth, development and repair of body tissues. Manganese in bananas is good for a healthy and glowing skin. This fruit is also rich in potassium which is good for heart health and blood pressure. Bananas also aids digestion and help beat gastrointestinal issues. Above evrerything else, bananas give you energy – minus the fats and cholesterol​.

Avocado is the only fruit that provides a substantial amount of healthy monounsaturated fatty acids. Avocados are a great source of vitamins C, E, K, and B-6. It also contains omega-3 fatty acids which help lubricate joints and releive joit pain. According to the book of Healing foods, ovacados helps in boosting fertility and better the chances of conceiving too. Including avocados in your diet may help improve eyesight. Avocados are loaded with beta-carotene and antioxidants which are beneficial for eyes.
